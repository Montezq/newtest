The Metaverse Store is the Genesis concept collection by Futureology. We're a futurist art and storytelling collective exploring new visions of futures, with the goal of helping create a future which excites us, and one which belongs to everyone.

The Metaverse Store - EST 2042 - is part pop up store, part NFT art installation and part imagined futures inquiry into what might surprise or terrify us about the future of the evolving metaverse. Featuring over 80 items from a future metaverse ranging from Injectible emotions to virtual camping supplies and consciousness shifting eyewear this collection explores a world where we live with ‘virtual beings’, and have augmented our brains to directly interface with the metaverse. What might these objects tell us about the world in 2042?

This genesis collection serves as a passport to a journey of future worldbuilding on reality bending roadmap of possibility. Changemakers, join us for the ride.
